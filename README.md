Just a student who treats coding as more than a skill—it’s a passion and a professional hobby. When I’m not immersed in lines of code, you’ll probably find me enjoying my ultimate comfort meal: Indomie with eggs.

I’ve always admired Iron Man—not just because of the suit, but because of the mindset of building something extraordinary from a simple garage. That vision inspires my own journey. My dream is to build a startup from a small room, grow it through innovation and persistence, and one day stand alongside—or even surpass—the biggest names in the tech industry.

For me, every bug is an opportunity to learn, every project is a step forward, and every line of code brings me closer to turning ambitious ideas into reality.
